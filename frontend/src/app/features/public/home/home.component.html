Welcome to K.B.S.S

Kuranooooo Malankpaka!

At Kissi Bendu Secondary School, we believe that every student carries within them the potential to achieve greatness. Our mission is to unlock that potential through quality education, strong values, and a nurturing environment.

Kissi Bendu believes in Pragmatism — we don't just talk about education, we put it into action. Every lesson, every activity, and every achievement here is grounded in real, practical results that shape the lives of our students and the future of our community.

Established in the heart of Koindu Town, K.B.S.S has been a pillar of educational excellence in Kailahun District for over four decades. We are committed to providing our students with the knowledge, skills, and character needed to thrive in today's ever-changing world.

I invite you to join our school community — whether as a student, parent, or partner. Together, we will continue to build a brighter future for Sierra Leone.

Wangdo F. Bundor
Principal, Kissi Bendu Secondary School
